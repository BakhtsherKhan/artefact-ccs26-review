# Ethics and Redaction Notes

This artifact was prepared for double-blind review and for responsible scientific evaluation of a controlled security experiment.

## Controlled Environment

All experiments were conducted using author-controlled devices, accounts, and network infrastructure. The study did not involve third-party users, public traffic interception, credential theft, phishing, malware deployment, or unauthorized access to external systems.

## Redaction

The artifact excludes or removes:

- author names and affiliations;
- personal GitHub links;
- private account identifiers;
- authentication material;
- raw private logs;
- unsanitized screenshots;
- unsanitized personal traffic;
- operational exploit scripts.

## Withheld Materials

Executable sender-side replay and mutation scripts are not included. These materials are withheld to reduce misuse risk because they could enable operational abuse against a real messaging application.

## Released Materials

The artifact releases representative packet traces, sanitized documentation, and CryptoVerif models. These materials are sufficient to evaluate the paper's core empirical and formal claims without exposing private data or enabling operational exploitation.
